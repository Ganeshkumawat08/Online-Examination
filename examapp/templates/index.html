Online Examination

Online examination is a modern technique of conducting exams through the internet using digital devices like computers, laptops, or mobile phones. It has become very popular in today's digital era as it makes the process of giving and taking exams easier and more efficient.

Key Features:

  • Remote Access from anywhere
  • Automatic evaluation (especially for MCQs)
  • Time-limited tests
  • Secure login and proctoring options

Benefits:

  • Saves time and effort
  • Cost-effective
  • Instant result generation
  • Environmentally friendly

Challenges:

  • Internet dependency
  • Technical issues
  • Risk of cheating if not properly monitored

Overall, online examinations are a safe, fast, and convenient method of assessment in the digital world. With the help of proper technology and monitoring, they are shaping the future of education and evaluation.
